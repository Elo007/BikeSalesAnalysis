# Bike Sales Analysis


 In this project, we built a dashbord in Excel to analyze the sale of 481 bikes across Europe, North America and the Pacific.
 

# Insights


* Sales by gender 
* Sales by country
* Sales by profession
* Sales by age group of customers
* Average income of male vs female customers
